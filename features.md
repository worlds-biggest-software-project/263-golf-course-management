# Golf Course Management — Feature & Functionality Survey

> Candidate #263 · Researched: 2026-05-03

## Cross-Cutting Feature Themes

### Table-Stakes Features

- Tee sheet and booking management
- Pro shop and F&B POS
- Member/player CRM and profiles
- Dynamic pricing and revenue management
- Reporting and analytics
- Integration with GHIN/WHS for handicaps
- Payment processing
- Mobile app for players

### Differentiating Features

- Marketplace integration (GolfNow tee time distribution)
- Dynamic pricing engine
- Loyalty and membership management
- Tournament management and scoring
- Integrated marketing tools
- Food & beverage integration
- Channel distribution and rate management
- AI-driven demand forecasting

### AI-Augmentation Candidates

- Dynamic pricing based on demand and weather
- Predictive course utilisation and revenue
- Personalised player recommendations
- Automated marketing and retention campaigns
- Course conditioning and maintenance prediction

## Recommended Feature Scope

**Must-have (MVP)**:
- Tee sheet and booking management
- Pro shop and F&B POS
- Player CRM and profiles
- GHIN/WHS handicap integration
- Payment processing
- Reporting and analytics

**Should-have (v1.1)**:
- Dynamic pricing engine
- Membership billing and loyalty
- Tournament management tools
- Marketing automation
- Marketplace integration (GolfNow)
- Predictive demand forecasting

**Nice-to-have (backlog)**:
- AI-driven course optimisation
- Advanced tournament systems
- Integrated golf media and content
- Mobile player app
- Sustainability tracking
